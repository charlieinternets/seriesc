Funding the future.

seriesc connects entrepreneurs to capital. Founders simply choose a name/identity for their venture, and a capital raise vehicle is minted and plugged into the distributed web instantaneously.

How it works:

seriesc harnesses the power of The Ethereum Name Service, and subdomains within. When you sign up with seriesc, we provide you with a unique "name.seriesc.eth" identity, which will be minted to the Ethereum blockchain as a ERC1155 token.
